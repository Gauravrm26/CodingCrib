Events By Coding Crib

BMS Institute of Technology

We conduct Coding events like MCQ Quizzes on programming, Debugging Contests in various programming languages like Java, C, C++, etc. Problem Statements will be provided to our coding community on a regular basis and they have to come up with their best and executable solutions. Workshops and interactive sessions with Industry Expert, Alumni, Coders, Hackers and mentors are organized to keep the student coding community always engaged with the latest technological trends and also to gain valuable knowledge. We conduct Hackathons that are proving platform for cracking the creativity code and also act as an ignite for innovation. We help our student programmers gain more insights in coding and hone their technical skills.
